About

My name is Agelos Kratimenos and I am a senior student in the field of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA) in Greece.

My main areas of interest are Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and specifically Computer Vision, Robotics, and Deep Reinforcement Learning.

I am currently working as a Research Assistant in the Control Systems Laboratory (CSL-EP) in MECH NTUA while completing my Master Thesis on 3D Body, Face and Hands Reconstruction in the CVSP Laboratory in ECE NTUA.

Professional and Research Experience

08/2019 – Current

CSL-EP Labaratory
Undergraduate Research Assistant, Control Systems Lab, MECH NTUA

Currently working on creating a real-time robotic application for following, identifying and grabbing grape clusters in a vineyard for the HexaTerra Legged Robot.

Experimenting with state-of-the-art methods for object detection, i.e. Mask-RCNN and Yolo-v3, while exploiting Cycle-GANS for applying de-raining image recovery to make the system robust.
